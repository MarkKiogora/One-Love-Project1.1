About Us

The One Love Project is a charitable initiative aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goal of Zero Hunger. We work to bring communities together through fundraising, donations, and educational outreach.

Our Mission

To provide consistent food, clothing, and resources for children’s homes while empowering communities with knowledge on hunger reduction.

Quick Facts

  • Founded: 2023
  • Focus: Zero Hunger
  • Community reach: 500+ families
